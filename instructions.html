Racheal Ray's At Home Dog Biscuits

These might be the best dog biscuits your dog will ever snack on

By Rachel Hofer

Directions

Direction Image or Example
Combine all ingredients in a bowl
You may have to use your hands as it will be very dry
Pat it onto a cookie sheet to about 1/2 in thick
Cut into shapes of your choice
You can let dry or bake in a 350 degree F oven until firm, about 10-15 mintues
